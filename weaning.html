Feia Weaning

This is a mobile app, made with React Native. It is currently available on the market (Android and iOS)

It is another family oriented FEIA application. Weaning is developed in cooperation with physicians and dieticians to be a helpful assistant in the process of weaning. It contains over a 100 weaning recipes from 4 to 36+ months old babies.

I worked on various, front end components of this app. My main task was to put together different screens displaying recipes, following the designs. I also implemented several features, related to navigating through the app.
